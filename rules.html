Vehicle Setup

Configure your race car's performance:

Max Speed 100-350 km/h Sets the top velocity reachable on long straights.
Acceleration 10-100 rating Determines how quickly the engine pushes the car forward.
Cornering Grip 0.1-1.0 scale Traction available to maintain high speed through turns.
Braking Power 2x-15x mult. Efficiency of the brakes when slowing for tight hairpins.
Aero Downforce 1.0-2.0 range Uses airflow to "stick" the car to the track at speed.
Grip vs Drag Trade-off Balancing cornering agility against straight-line pace.
🏁

Tire Compounds

Choose the right compound for your race strategy:

  • Soft (Red) — +0.18 grip, 10% wear/lap. Max grip, fastest but wears quickly.
  • Medium (Yellow) — +0.08 grip, 4% wear/lap. Balanced performance.
  • Hard (White) — -0.04 grip, 1.5% wear/lap. Lower grip but durable.
  • Intermediate (Green) — +0.12 grip, 8% wear/lap. Perfect for light rain.
  • Full Wet (Blue) — +0.18 grip, 5% wear/lap. For heavy rain conditions.

Tire health affects grip. Do not use wet tires in the dry!

Pit Stops (Boxing):

  • Button appears dynamically if tire health < 60%.
  • Alert: Button pulses red and engineer calls "Box, box!" if tires are critical (<30%).
  • Click PIT STOP (BOX) or a new compound mid-race to queue a stop.
  • Tire health resets to 100% when crossing the line.
  • A 12-second penalty is added (3s tire change + 9s pit lane travel).

Race Engineer Analysis

How the Engineer helps you find the Sweet Spot:

  1. Profiling: Track geometry is measured to determine its "DNA" (e.g. High-Speed vs Technical).
  2. Golden Setup: A perfect numerical target is calculated for every circuit.
  3. Comparison: Real-time tracking of your settings vs the ideal targets.
  4. Smart Advice: Engineer identifies if you are under-tuned, perfect, or over-tuned.

Enable 📻 Radio to start receiving engineer feedback.

Simulation Controls

Keyboard shortcuts:

  • SPACE — Launch/reset simulation
  • C — Cycle camera modes
  • S — Toggle engine sound
  • H — Toggle UI (3 modes)

UI Modes:

  • Mode 0: All panels visible
  • Mode 1: Stats only (immersive)
  • Mode 2: Hidden + floating HUD

Physics & Simulation

Realistic racing physics:

  • Grip Model: Base + compound - wear + downforce
  • Rain: 40% grip reduction
  • Aero Drag: Downforce vs speed trade-off
  • Dynamic Speed: Auto-slows for corners
  • Visuals: Pitch, roll, steering respond to physics
  • Shift Lights: RPM indicators near max speed

Pro Tips

  • Watch Health: Below 30% impacts lap times

  • Radio Engineer: Setup advice and lap summaries
